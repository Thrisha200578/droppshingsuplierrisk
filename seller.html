📊 How Risk Scores Work

What the badge on each manufacturer means

0–35
Low Risk ✓
Reliable, high-rated, consistent fulfilment. Safe to order.
36–65
Medium Risk ⚠
Some inconsistencies. Review their history before ordering.
66–100
High Risk ✗
Frequent issues flagged. Proceed with caution.
Score is calculated from: Rating (40%) + AI Sentiment Analysis (30%) + Fulfilment Rate (20%) + Low Refund Rate (10%). New manufacturers without any orders show No Score — they aren't necessarily risky, just unrated.
