✍️ Tips for a High-Performing Ad

Ads that convert well share these traits

✅ Be Specific
Name the exact products you make. "Precision injection-moulded plastic components for consumer electronics" beats "Plastic parts".
✅ Mention Your MOQ
Dropshippers need to know if they can afford to order from you. State your minimum order quantity upfront.
✅ Add a Lead Time
Tell them how fast you can ship. Fast lead times are a major advantage and a key reason dropshippers choose one manufacturer over another.
✅ Offer a First-Order Incentive
New accounts convert better with a small incentive like a discount or free sample. Use the Offer field to highlight this.
